How to make a dessert pizza

Materials needed

  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of Italian breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • String cheese, 1 for every 2 you want to make (cut them in half)
  • Stovetop and frying pan

how to... Make it!

  1. Whisk eggs and milk together.
  2. Put flour on your pieces of cheese.
  3. Dip in egg-milk mixture.
  4. roll in breadcrumbs.
  5. Freeze for 6-24 hours. (this is going to take a long time)
  6. Heat your vegetable oil on your pan.
  7. Cook sticks for about a minute on each side.
  8. DON'T FORGET TO TURN OFF THE OVEN!!!
  9. OPTIONAL: Serve with marinara sauce.
