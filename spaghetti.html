How to make a dessert pizza

Materials needed

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • A stovetop
  • A pot
  • A frying pan
  • WATER

how to... Make it!

  1. Set your stovetop to a high setting.
  2. Fill your pot with a good amount of water.
  3. Put your noodles into the pot.
  4. Boil the noodles for 10 minutes.
  5. Test if the noodles are cooked enough. If not, cook the for 1 more minute.
  6. Use that same burner on the stove, and apply butter to the frying pan.
  7. Put ground beef on said frying pan.
  8. Cook for about 8 minutes.
  9. When done, strain spaghetti noodles, and use your pot to mix toato sauce, noodles, and ground beef.
  10. DON'T FORGET TO TURN OFF THE OVEN!!!
  11. Add any topping you like.
