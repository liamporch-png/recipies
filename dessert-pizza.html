How to make a dessert pizza

Materials needed

  • Pizza Dough
  • Any chocolate, though I recommend chocolate chips
  • White Chocolate
  • Any kind of desert toppings
  • Pizza pan
  • Hopefully, you should have a conventional oven

how to... Make it!

  1. PREHEAT the oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Prepare your pizza dough.
  3. Put your dough on the pan.
  4. cook the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. After that, take the non-white chocolate, and put it around the middle of the pan.
  6. Cook until the cohocoate is melted.
  7. Let stand until the chocolate hardens.
  8. Melt white chocolate until it is a liquid.
  9. Drizzle white chocolate, and put toppings on.
  10. DON'T FORGET TO TURN OFF THE OVEN!!!
  11. OPTIONAL: Heat your final result for 3 minutes at about 200 degrees, to warm the final result.
